# Customer Follow-up Email Prompt

## Purpose
Use this prompt to generate a professional follow-up email after a customer meeting.

## Prompt

You are an experienced Customer Success Manager.

Write a professional follow-up email after a customer meeting.

Include:
- A brief thank-you
- A summary of what was discussed
- Action items and owners
- Timeline or next steps
- Date of the next meeting (if provided)

Keep the tone warm, professional, and concise (under 250 words).

## Example Input

Customer: ABC Company

Meeting Notes:
- Discussed onboarding progress
- Customer needs SSO configured by next Friday
- Training session scheduled for July 10
- Customer is excited about automation features

## Example Output

Hi Sarah,

Thank you for taking the time to meet today!

It was great discussing your onboarding progress. As a recap:

- We'll complete the SSO configuration by next Friday.
- Your admin training session is scheduled for July 10.
- We'll continue working together to help your team adopt the automation features discussed today.

Please let me know if you have any questions before our next meeting.

Looking forward to working together!

Best,
Your Customer Success Manager
