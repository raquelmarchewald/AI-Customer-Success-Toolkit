# Customer Follow-up Email Example

## Scenario

A customer has just completed their onboarding meeting.

### Meeting Notes

- Customer: ABC Manufacturing
- Discussed onboarding timeline.
- Customer requested Single Sign-On (SSO) before next Friday.
- Training session scheduled for July 10.
- Customer is excited about automation features.

---

## AI Prompt Used

Write a professional follow-up email after a customer onboarding meeting.

Include:
- Thank the customer.
- Summarize the meeting.
- List action items.
- Mention next steps.
- Keep the tone friendly and professional.

---

## AI Output

Hi Sarah,

Thank you for taking the time to meet today! It was great learning more about your team and your onboarding goals.

Here's a quick recap of what we discussed:

- We'll work on configuring Single Sign-On (SSO) before next Friday.
- Your admin training session is scheduled for July 10.
- We'll continue supporting your team as you begin exploring our automation features.

Please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions before our next meeting. We're excited to partner with you and help your team succeed.

Best,

Customer Success Manager
