AI Customer Success Toolkit

About this project

As a Software QA professional transitioning into Customer Success, I wanted to explore practical ways AI can help teams spend less time on repetitive work and more time helping customers succeed.

This repository contains prompts, templates, and workflow ideas I’ve created while learning how AI can improve customer communication, onboarding, documentation, and collaboration between Customer Success, Product, and Engineering teams.

Rather than replacing human interaction, I believe AI is most valuable when it helps professionals communicate more clearly, solve problems faster, and deliver a better customer experience.

⸻

What’s Included

* Customer onboarding prompts
* Customer email templates
* Meeting summary prompts
* Quarterly Business Review templates
* Bug report generation
* Feature request analysis
* Test case generation
* Workflow automation ideas
* AI productivity tips

Why I built this

Throughout my career in Software Quality Assurance, I’ve worked closely with developers, product managers, support teams, and customers to deliver better software.

As I transition into Customer Success, I wanted to combine my technical background with AI tools to create resources that help teams work smarter while keeping customers at the center of every decision.

This project reflects my curiosity, continuous learning mindset, and passion for improving processes through technology.
